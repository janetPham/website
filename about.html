Reliable. Creative. Open-minded.

I'm Janet and I'm a third-year undergrad studying mechatronics engineering at the University of Waterloo. I have a love for smart systems, sustainable design, and improving processes. I’ve led projects in lean manufacturing, quality systems, and automation across automotive and aerospace. For me, it's not just about what works—it's about what matters. My interest lies in pursuing how designs, processes, and systems integrate into people's lives. In short, I'm big on ownership, impact, and building systems where safety, clarity, and collaboration thrive!


Outside of school, I'm a fan of Formula 1 racing and I like to stack cards, cook, and collect vinyl records. I'm currently exploring sonic experimentation through the electric guitar and dialing amps to build genre sound profiles. My early experiments included 3D-printing picks, trying to play with a coin (not recommended), and synth workshops. Eventually, I'd like to DIY my own audio gear.
